# Naming

* **OS Name:** Cryptum
* **TUI WM Name:** Metarch

1. The "Monitor" Tier (Best for the OS/Kernel)

In Halo lore, Monitors are the clinical, efficient AIs that manage entire installations. This fits a minimal Alpine base perfectly.

* **Guilty Spark:** The most iconic monitor; implies a small, focused, yet powerful core.
* **Adjutant:** Short for *Adjutant Resolution* or *Adjutant Reflex*. It sounds like a "system helper" or a lightweight daemon.
* **032 Mendicant:** After *Mendicant Bias*. It implies a system that has "abandoned" the traditional GUI for something more direct.
* **Tragic Solitude:** A bit more "edgy" and poetic, perfect for a solitary terminal environment.
* **Penitent:** After *Penitent Tangent*; implies a stripped-back, "punishingly" minimal system.

1. The "Ancilla" Tier (Best for the TUI Window Manager)

An Ancilla is a Forerunner personal AI or a background system process. Since your WM manages TUI "windows," these names fit the "interface" role.

* **Ancilla:** The literal term for Forerunner AI; clean and professional.
* **Metarch:** The highest class of Forerunner AI (like *Mendicant Bias*). Great for a WM that "rules" all other TUI apps.
* **Abyss:** Derived from the *Abyssal* class of ancilla.
* **Constructor:** Named after the small, floating automated repair drones. Perfect for a WM that "builds" your layout.

1. "Architectural" Names (Best for the Project as a Whole)

Forerunners are known for their massive, clean, geometric structures.

* **Cryptum:** A Forerunner meditation pod or "stasis" chamber. It implies a contained, safe, and minimal environment.
* **Cartographer:** After the *Silent Cartographer*. Excellent if your TUI WM has a strong focus on navigation or search.
* **Ark:** The birthplace of the rings; fits a "base" OS that houses your other tools.
* **Maethrillian:** The Forerunner capital world. It sounds grand and foundational.
* **Ecumene:** The name of the Forerunner empire; a great umbrella name for your entire project suite.

1. Ship & Protocol Names (Short & Punchy)

* **Audacity:** The name of the Librarian's ship; bold and simple.
* **Mantle:** After the *Mantle of Responsibility*.
* **Keyship:** The ships used to activate the rings; implies "unlocking" potential or being the "key" to your workflow.
* **Sojourner:** A class of Forerunner dreadnought; implies a system meant for travel (portable/minimal).
